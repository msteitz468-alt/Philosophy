## Key Model and Techniques (from TOC + sampled chapters)

### The Alternatives Model / Space
- Infinite "alternatives space" contains scripts of all possible events (past/present/future).
- A person's thought energy can materialize a sector under certain conditions.
- Reality as "mirror of alternatives": you shape your reflection by changing the image (your thoughts/lifestyle), not by directly pushing the reflection (external effort/fighting).
- "Dreams don’t come true" in the old sense; instead, you select and glide to the desired sector.

### Pendulums
- Energy structures created by collective thought/emotion (e.g. epidemic panic, poverty, war, trends, corporations, ideologies).
- They "feed" on your energy when you "swing" with them (emotional involvement, importance).
- Rule: do not fight or hate the pendulum; reduce importance, detach, or "go with the flow" while directing your own intention. Attacking one feeds it.

### Importance, Excess Potential, Equilibrium
- Importance (over-valuing something) creates "excess potential" — distortion in the energy field that triggers balancing forces (problems, obstacles, "bad luck").
- Reduce importance → dissolve excess potential → equilibrium restored, path clears.
- "Letting go," "going with the flow," "guiding signs."

### Intention (Inner & Outer)
- Inner intention: "I want / I will achieve" — often creates excess potential or fights the mirror.
- Outer intention: "It is happening / the world is choosing this for me" or "I allow" — selects the sector without distortion. Magical power when purified.
- Slides: positive mental images of the goal or process (visualize the result as already here; avoid negative "anti-slides").
- The "game script" — you can write your own rules.

### Heart and Mind Unity ("The Wizard Within")
- Mind (logic, doubt, stereotypes) vs Heart (true desires, intuition).
- "Wind of Intention," "Heart’s Sail."
- Unity allows clean outer intention. "Guardian angels," "mirage," "soul box."
- Sound-bite slides, frames, "a window onto the alternatives space."

### Goals, Doors, Energy
- Choose *your own* goals (not others' or stereotypes); become a trendsetter.
- Energy levels, vampires, protective shield, "step of intention."
- Frailing (relationships context?), coordination of intention, "apples fall to the sky" (inversion when importance high?).
- "Forward to the past," "letters from the past," "dances with shadows," "dreams of the gods," "mirror world," "gatekeeper to eternity."

### Structure (from TOC excerpts)
Chapters cover: Alternatives Flow, Intention (lucid dreaming, outer intention, game script), Slides, Heart & Mind, Goals & Doors, Energy, Frailing, Coordination, Forward to the Past, Letters from the Past, Dances with Shadows, Dreams of the Gods, Mirror World, Gatekeeper to Eternity, plus Afterword, Glossary.
