## Life and Reception

Born 1947, Japanese-American; built 2.3 MeV betatron atom smasher in mother's garage as teen (400 lbs transformer steel, 22 miles wire; blew fuses). Early cloud chamber antimatter photos. PhD Harvard; co-founded string field theory. CUNY professor. TV host (BBC, Discovery, Science Channel) profiling labs/prototypes. Books bestsellers; makes physics accessible while forecasting. Reception: praised for scientist interviews/grounding in laws (vs. pure speculation); some critique optimism or specific timelines (e.g., AI). Influences public understanding of string theory, future tech, SETI/Kardashev.
