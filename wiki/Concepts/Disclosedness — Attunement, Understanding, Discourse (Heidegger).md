# Disclosedness — Attunement, Understanding, Discourse (Heidegger)
> Dasein *is* its "there": the openness in which world, others, and self are disclosed — constituted equiprimordially by mood, projective understanding, and discourse.

## Summary

*Being and Time* §§28–34. **Disclosedness** (*Erschlossenheit*) is Dasein's basic character of *being its there* — the clearing in which anything can show up at all. It is the most primordial phenomenon of truth (see [[Concepts/Truth as Unconcealment (Heidegger)]]) and is constituted by three equiprimordial existentials:

### Attunement (Befindlichkeit) — §§29–30

Mood discloses Dasein's **thrownness** (*Geworfenheit*) — "this character of being of Dasein which is veiled in its whence and whither, but in itself all the more openly disclosed, this 'that it is'" (§29) — *prior to* all cognition and willing. Mood first makes it possible that anything *matters*; a world-openness without attunement is impossible. Fear (§30) is analyzed as a template: what we fear (an approaching innerworldly threat), the fearing itself, and that *about* which we fear (Dasein itself). Its ontologically deeper sibling is [[Concepts/Anxiety (Angst) (Heidegger)|anxiety]].

### Understanding (Verstehen) and Interpretation — §§31–33

Understanding is not a cognitive faculty but Dasein's being as **potentiality-of-being**: its structure is **project** (*Entwurf*), the projecting of Dasein upon possibilities *as* possibilities — not a thought-out plan. Dasein is "thrown possibility," a *thrown project*: "Dasein is constantly 'more' than it actually is … it is never more than it factically is"; hence it "can say understandingly to itself: 'become what you are!'" (§31). Intuition and thought are "remote derivatives of understanding."

**Interpretation** (*Auslegung*) develops understanding by making explicit the "something *as* something," always on the basis of the **fore-structure**: fore-having, fore-sight, fore-conception (§32). Hence the **hermeneutic circle** is no *circulus vitiosus*: "What is decisive is not to get out of the circle, but to get in it in the right way. This circle of understanding is … the expression of the existential fore-structure of Dasein itself" (§32). Meaning (*Sinn*) is an existential of Dasein, not a property of things.

**Statement** (*Aussage*) — pointing out + predication + communication — is an "extreme derivative" of interpretation: it levels the existential-hermeneutical "as" down to the *apophantical* "as," converting the handy into the merely objectively present (§33). Traditional logic and the theory of judgment therefore rest on a derivative mode of disclosure — the ontological root of Heidegger's demotion of assertion-truth.

### Discourse (Rede) — §34

Discourse — the articulation of intelligibility — is equiprimordial with attunement and understanding, and is the existential-ontological *foundation* of language (*Sprache*): "words accrue to significations," not the reverse. Listening and keeping silent are constitutive possibilities of discourse. Its everyday degenerate form is idle talk (*Gerede*) — see [[Concepts/The They (das Man) and Falling Prey (Heidegger)]].

### Temporal Grounding (§68)

Division Two re-derives each moment: understanding temporalizes primarily out of the **future** (authentic: anticipation; inauthentic: awaiting), attunement out of **having-been** (fear is "a forgetting that awaits and makes present"; anxiety holds the Moment in readiness), falling prey out of the **present** (making-present), and discourse articulates all three. See [[Concepts/Temporality (Heidegger)]].

## Key Claims / Positions

### How This Compares to Other Thinkers in the Wiki

- **[[Thinkers/Edmund Husserl]]**: perception and judgment ground meaning in intentional consciousness; Heidegger grounds them in pre-cognitive attunement and practical projection — moods are not "coloring" on neutral perception but the prior condition of anything mattering.
- **[[Thinkers/Descartes]] / rationalism**: cognition is demoted to a founded mode; the clear and distinct judgment presupposes a disclosed world it can never reconstruct.
- **[[Thinkers/Ludwig Wittgenstein]]** (where present): the priority of discourse over language and of practice over assertion parallels the later Wittgenstein's meaning-as-use — a standard comparative observation, flagged here for future linking.

## Contradictions / Open Questions

- > [!warning] If statement-truth is derivative, the status of *Being and Time*'s own assertions becomes delicate — Heidegger's "violent" existential interpretation (§63) concedes the point.
- The equiprimordiality of the three existentials sits uneasily with §65's claim that the future is *primary* in temporality.
