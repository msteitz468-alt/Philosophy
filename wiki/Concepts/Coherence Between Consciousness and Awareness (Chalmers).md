# Coherence Between Consciousness and Awareness (Chalmers)
> Where there is consciousness there is awareness — "direct availability for global control" — and the structure of each mirrors the other; the first, nonbasic psychophysical law.

## Summary

*The Conscious Mind* ch. 6 begins the constructive program: a **nonreductive theory** whose psychophysical laws "will be on a par with the laws of physics as part of the basic furniture of the universe." It explains the distribution and structure of experience, not its existence; some bruteness remains, as with ultimate physical laws. Method: first-person regularities plus plausibility constraints (simplicity, reliability of report), by inference to the best explanation.

The central **coherence principle**: consciousness goes with awareness, refined as *direct availability for global control* — dropping strict reportability (so animals qualify) and excluding nonoccurrent knowledge. The **principle of structural coherence**: "the structure of consciousness is mirrored by the structure of awareness, and the structure of awareness is mirrored by the structure of consciousness" — the geometry of the visual field, 3-D color space, intensity, similarity relations. Blindsight, dreams, and Block's access/phenomenal cases refine rather than refute it.

This yields a **dualist (nonreductive) functionalism**: awareness suffices for consciousness by (nonbasic) law; any extra "X-factor" (biology, no-homunculi) is arbitrary — "a holdover from the attempt to have one's materialist cake and eat one's consciousness too." Against Dennett's "cerebral celebrity," only *potential* celebrity is required; against Rosenthal/Lycan higher-order theories, first-order registrations fit better. And coherence is the **epistemic lever** behind all NCC research: "We can only have empirical evidence for a link between a process N and consciousness if we already have evidence for a link between N and awareness" — Crick & Koch's 40-Hz, Baars's global workspace, and Libet are candidate *mechanisms of global availability*, derivative on the overarching law.

## How Different Thinkers Use This

- **[[Thinkers/Daniel Dennett]]**: "Consciousness is cerebral celebrity—nothing more and nothing less" — Chalmers keeps the functional story as the correlate while denying it exhausts the phenomenon.
- **Baars / Crick & Koch**: their models, dismissed as answers to the hard problem in ch. 3, are rehabilitated here as legitimate science *of awareness* — the charitable half of Chalmers's dialectic.
- **[[Thinkers/Andy Clark]]** ([[Concepts/Predictive Processing (Clark)]]): predictive-processing accounts of access and precision are, in Chalmers's terms, theories of awareness; the coherence principle says why they still constrain a theory of consciousness.
- Extends downward — dogs, mice, arguably flies — preparing the panpsychist slide of [[Concepts/Double-Aspect Theory of Information (Chalmers)]].
