# Confirmation Holism (Duhem-Quine Thesis)

> Our statements about the external world face the tribunal of sense experience not individually but only as a corporate body. (Quine, "Two Dogmas of Empiricism"). The unit of empirical significance is the whole of science (or at least large chunks of the web of belief), not the isolated statement or even the isolated theory. Any contrary experience can in principle be accommodated by adjustments anywhere in the system.

## Core Statement (Quine)

- Total science (or our "knowledge or beliefs" from casual geography to atomic physics, mathematics, and logic) is a **man-made fabric** that impinges on experience only at the edges.
- A conflict with experience at the periphery occasions **readjustments in the interior** of the field. Truth values have to be redistributed over some of our statements.
- Re-evaluation of some statements entails re-evaluation of others because of their logical interconnections (the logical laws themselves are further statements in the system).
- The total field is so underdetermined by its boundary conditions (experience) that there is much **latitude of choice** as to which statements to re-evaluate in light of any single contrary experience.
- No particular experiences are linked with any particular statements in the interior of the field, except indirectly through considerations of equilibrium affecting the field as a whole.

Physical objects, small and large, are posits introduced to simplify our treatment of experience; they are not reducible to it. The same holds for forces, abstract entities, classes, and even (epistemologically) the gods of Homer. All are cultural posits whose justification is pragmatic.

Quine credits Pierre Duhem (*La Théorie physique*, 1906) with the argument that the physicist's system as a whole faces experiment, not individual hypotheses in isolation.

## Consequences

- It is misleading (or nonsense) to speak of the empirical content of an *individual* statement, especially one remote from the experiential periphery.
- Any statement can be held true come what may, if we make drastic enough adjustments elsewhere (including changes to logical laws).
- Conversely, no statement is immune to revision. Revision even of the law of the excluded middle has been proposed to simplify quantum mechanics.
- The distinction between statements held by convention and statements held on the basis of observation of nature is one of degree (how centrally located in the web; how preferentially connected to particular experiences), not of kind.
- Ontological questions (e.g., whether to countenance classes as entities, i.e., to quantify over variables taking classes as values) are on a par with questions of natural science. Carnap's attempt to treat them as "choice of conceptual scheme" while keeping scientific hypotheses factual relies on the analytic/synthetic distinction that Quine rejects.

## Relation to Vault Content and Thinkers

- **Duhem**: Explicitly cited by Quine for the holism point. Duhem is the historical source of the "Duhem-Quine thesis."
- **Hume / Problem of Induction**: Holism radicalizes Hume's problem. It is not merely that we lack a rational foundation for projecting past regularities; even the "empirical content" that would be projected is not locally assignable to particular statements or laws.
- **Popper**: Popper's methodology assumes that specific theories can be severely tested and potentially falsified by basic statements. Holism suggests that any apparent falsification can be absorbed by adjustments to auxiliaries, background assumptions, or even logic. This is a central productive tension between Quinean holism and Popperian falsificationism (see [[Concepts/Falsifiability as Criterion of Demarcation (Popper)]] and cross-references on the Popper thinker page).
- **Kuhn**: Kuhn's normal science (puzzle-solving within a paradigm that is presupposed, not tested) and the non-compelled nature of paradigm shifts have clear affinities with holism and underdetermination. Incommensurability (shift in meanings, problems, standards) is one manifestation of the web's resistance to piecemeal comparison. See [[Thinkers/Thomas Kuhn]] and the dedicated [[Contradictions/Popper vs Kuhn - Falsification vs Paradigms]].
- **Feyerabend**: takes underdetermination + theory-ladenness to an anarchist conclusion — if any statement can be held against the data by relocating the conflict (holism), then [[Concepts/Counterinduction (Feyerabend)|counterinduction]] and [[Concepts/Proliferation and Tenacity (Feyerabend)|tenacity]] are legitimate and "[[Concepts/Epistemological Anarchism (Feyerabend)|anything goes]]." Quine draws a naturalist-pragmatist moral from the same premise where Feyerabend draws a pluralist-anarchist one. See [[Thinkers/Paul Feyerabend]], [[Sources/Against Method - Paul Feyerabend]].
- **Russell / Correspondence**: Quine's holism and posit-myth language sit in tension with Russell's more atomistic sense-data realism and correspondence theory (though both are anti-pragmatist in important respects). The vault already carries Russell's "cosmic impiety" warning; Quine offers a more thorough naturalist/pragmatist alternative.

## Relevance to Vault

- **The wiki itself as holistic web**: A new raw source is experience at the edge. Integration is never a local verification or falsification of one page; it requires (or at least permits) readjustment of truth-values across many linked pages (cross-references, frontmatter sources:, Related sections, index lines, Overview history, even schema emphasis). The "latitude of choice" is real: multiple coherent ways to absorb a source exist; the agent chooses on grounds of conservatism, simplicity, future utility, and explanatory power.
- **Lint and contradictions as critical pressure**: They surface the "recalcitrant experiences" that the current web cannot easily accommodate without redistribution. Silent patching (ad hoc auxiliaries) is the Quinean vice to avoid.
- **Underdetermination in practice**: For any non-trivial question, multiple syntheses (different distributions of emphasis across sources) can be consistent with the "data." The vault's preference for explicit wikilinks, provenance, and flagged tensions is a methodological rule that keeps the web testable and revisable.
- **AI / models**: LLMs are massive holistic systems. "Grounding" or "retrieval" never pins an individual output to a unique experiential base; the whole context + parameters + prompt web is adjusted. Hallucination can be seen as an over-confident local assignment of empirical content that the web as a whole does not support.

**Open questions**:
- How much holism is too much? Can we preserve localized testing for observation sentences or for highly peripheral claims while accepting holism for theoretical interiors? (Quine's later work on observation sentences and "the external world" attempts degrees of localization.)
- What are the precise pragmatic criteria for choosing which strands to adjust? (Conservatism, simplicity, fruitfulness — but these themselves stand in need of articulation and can conflict.)
- Does holism plus naturalism leave any room for a normative epistemology that is not just descriptive psychology of science?
