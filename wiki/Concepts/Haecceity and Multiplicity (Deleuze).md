# Haecceity and Multiplicity (Deleuze)
> A haecceity is a mode of individuation irreducible to persons, subjects, or substances — it consists entirely of relations of movement/rest, speeds/slownesses, and capacities to affect and be affected.

## Summary

Deleuze borrows the medieval term *haecceitas* (Duns Scotus) to name a form of individuation that does not presuppose a subject. A haecceity has no inside or outside, no subject or object — only **longitude** (relations of movement and rest between particles) and **latitude** (capacities to affect and be affected in a zone of intensity). Five o'clock in the afternoon, a temperature, a pack of wolves — these are haecceities. They individuate without forming a substance.

This corresponds to the two planes Deleuze distinguishes:
- **Plane of forms and subjects** (governed by Chronos: development, memory, personal identity).
- **Plane of consistency** (governed by Aeon: haecceities, speeds, affects, becomings).

The semiotic of haecceity uses **indefinite article + proper name + infinitive verb**: "a child," "a woman," "to run," "to love." Here the proper name does not name a person but an event or becoming; the indefinite article is not indeterminate but indicates an immanent singular.

**Multiplicity** is a related claim at the level of structure rather than individuation. True multiplicities are not multiple ones (aggregates of identities) but change in *nature* as connections increase. They have dimensions but no supplementary unity. They are made by subtraction: n − 1. See [[Concepts/Rhizome vs Trees (Deleuze-Guattari)]].

## Key Claims

- "You are longitude and latitude, a set of speeds and slownesses between unformed particles, a set of nonsubjectified affects."
- Haecceity individuation: entirely on the plane of consistency. No transcendent identity, no developmental form, no mnemonic continuity.
- "Creations are like mutant abstract lines that have detached themselves from the task of representing a world."
- Multilinear assemblage: music and painting create by drawing a "diagonal" or transversal that breaks free from coordinates (harmony/melody, form/content) — a deterritorialized block that carries becoming.

## How Different Thinkers Use This

- **[[Thinkers/Aristotle]]:** Aristotle's primary substance is the individual thing (this man, this horse). Deleuze retains individuation but strips it of any substratum, essence, or form. What individuates a haecceity is nothing but its relational profile of speeds and affects.
- **[[Thinkers/Heidegger]] (Dasein):** Dasein is individuated by being-in-the-world, care, and being-toward-death — essentially temporal and structured around a "mineness." Deleuze's haecceity has no "mine" — it is impersonal, pre-individual, and distributed across a plane of consistency.
- **[[Thinkers/Pythagoras]]:** Distant structural resonance — Pythagorean number as intensive magnitude (not counting but ratio) anticipates Deleuze's longitude/latitude. But Pythagoras still posits number as essence of a determinate reality; Deleuze's multiplicities resist any final determination.
