# Alliant Insurance Services

> One of the largest insurance brokerages in the US. Key employer and target context for career/sales strategy in agribusiness / P&C lines, Central Valley / Western US operations (Fresno + Bakersfield offices).

## Key Facts from 2026 Sources
- **Agribusiness Division**: Dedicated team of 55+ specialists across crop, livestock, dairy, food processing, agtech verticals. Largest dedicated ag team in US.
- **Local Presence Advantage**: Fresno and Bakersfield offices enable "30 minutes away, not 3 hours" claims advocacy and same-day site visits.
- **Proprietary Programs**: Captive & alternative risk programs (group captives for nuts, dairy, vegetables, poultry) — typically 15–25% total-cost-of-risk savings at scale for clients.
- **Field-to-Fork Expertise**: Food safety, FSMA/LGMA compliance, product recall, supply-chain interruption, environmental liability (SGMA, CEQA, water curtailment). Strong in "from farm to fork" risk consulting.
- **Role in Career Context**: Current employer (20+ years for Mack Steitz as AM). Primary internal promotion target for Sr AE role with book ownership. Also the operational context for the agribusiness sales playbook (prospecting in Central Valley/Western US, using Alliant differentiators in every touch).

## Differentiators (to Anchor Outreach/Proposals/Renewals)
- Scale + specialization in ag (55+ team).
- True local service (same-day, nearby offices).
- Captives delivering measurable savings.
- End-to-end vertical expertise (compliance, recall, environmental, supply chain).
