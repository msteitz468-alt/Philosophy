## Study Materials (2026-06-04 ingest)
Added detailed CPCU 552 / commercial property series (The Institutes curriculum):
- Core glossary/FC with key terms (liability loss exposure, strict liability, hold-harmless/indemnity, mobile equipment, damages, dram shop act, etc.).
- 7 modules: Assessing the Need for Commercial Property Products (risk consultant role, BPP + BIC + endorsements), Recommending Building and Personal Property, Surviving Income Losses (EML, business income worksheet, coinsurance, peak periods, extra expenses — Peg's Toy Store fire cautionary tale), Tailoring Commercial Coverage, Closing Coverage Gaps (NFIP for floods, SFHAs, natural disaster gaps), Protecting Property in Transit, Critical thinking about Property Loss Exposures.
- 6 workbooks with exercises and application scenarios.

These directly build the technical depth for commercial (agribusiness) clients: property in transit for supply chains, business income for seasonal/processing operations, tailoring for food safety/environmental risks, and proactive gap analysis.
