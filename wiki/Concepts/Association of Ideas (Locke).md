# Association of Ideas (Locke)

> Besides NATURAL correspondence and connexion between ideas (which reason traces), CUSTOM or CHANCE creates "wrong connexion" of ideas not allied by nature. Such ideas "always keep in company"; "the one no sooner at any time comes into the understanding, but its associate appears with it"; "the whole gang, always inseparable, show themselves together." This "strong combination of ideas, not allied by nature, the mind makes in itself either voluntarily or by chance."

Leads to unreasonable opinions, obstinacy, antipathies, errors in moral and natural actions/passions/reasonings/notions — "a degree of madness" in most men (opposition to reason). Examples: goblins/sprites with darkness (from childhood inculcation); injury with the man (aversion transfers); pain/sickness with a place. "This wrong connexion in our minds of ideas in themselves loose and independent of one another, has such an influence... that perhaps there is not any one thing that deserves more to be looked after."

Education: those with charge of children should "diligently to watch, and carefully to prevent the undue connexion of ideas in the minds of young people" (most susceptible of lasting impressions).
