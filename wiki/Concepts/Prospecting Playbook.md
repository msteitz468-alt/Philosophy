# Prospecting Playbook

> Trigger-based, association-driven, multi-touch system for identifying and converting prospects into clients in specialty insurance brokerage (agribusiness focus). Core of the cradle-to-grave sales lifecycle in the Alliant Agribusiness playbook.

## Key Elements
- **Triggers**: Monitor for events creating needs (expansions, regulatory changes like FSMA/LGMA/SGMA/CEQA/water curtailment, weather/supply chain disruptions, new facilities, leadership changes, public news). Immediate tailored outreach upon trigger.
- **Associations & Events**: Key ag/food processing trade groups, local chambers (Central Valley/Western US), trade shows. Plan presence, speaking, sponsorships for visibility and warm intros.
- **Outreach**: Multi-touch sequences (email, phone, in-person, value-add content like risk assessments or captive savings examples). Scripts for first touch, follow-ups, requests for warm intros. Integrate prior research.
- **Target Identification**: Vertical deep-dives (crop/livestock/dairy/nuts/veg/food processing/agtech); premium potential; fit with Alliant capabilities (captives, local service, field-to-fork expertise); accessibility.
- **Metrics Tie-in**: Feeds the action calendar and pipeline (target 40-60 discovery meetings leading to 20-30 proposals / 10-15 bound accounts in 12 months).

## In Broader Strategy
Part of full lifecycle: Prospecting → Discovery & Needs Analysis (uncover full exposures beyond stated ask) → Proposal (anchor on 1-2 Alliant differentiators + client data) → Closing/Binding → Onboarding → Retention/Renewal/Cross-Sell (proactive gap ID, market updates, 2.5+ lines/client).
