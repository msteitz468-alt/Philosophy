# Evidence of Computation (Virk)

> Virk's empirical program: the simulation hypothesis is testable by hunting for computational artifacts in physics — rendering optimizations, pixel lattices, checksums, shared memory, and recursive algorithms.

## Summary

Against the charge of unfalsifiability, Virk catalogs proposed evidence of computation, conceding no single experiment is definitive:

| Category | Evidence | Figures cited |
|---|---|---|
| Conditional rendering | Delayed-choice results (Matera Laser Ranging Observatory); observer-dependent collapse | Wheeler, Campbell, Owhadi |
| Pixels / lattice | GZK cosmic-ray cutoff as lattice-spacing signature | Beane, Davoudi, Savage |
| Error-correcting codes | "Doubly-even self-dual linear binary error-correcting block codes" found in supersymmetry equations | James Gates |
| Entanglement as shared memory | Entangled particles as one memory address; error-correcting codes possibly defining space-time fabric | Almheiri, Dong, Harlow |
| Fractals | Self-similar recursion pervading nature — the algorithmic way to generate natural form | Mandelbrot |
| Simple programs | Nature as recursive algorithms, not continuous equations; computational irreducibility | Wolfram, *A New Kind of Science* |

**The skeptics' resource argument** (Ringel & Kovrizhin; Rucker): simulating even a few hundred particles exactly would require more atoms than the universe contains. **Virk's reply**: "video games are optimized to render only that which is needed. The rest of the 'world' exists in some informational form, so computing resources aren't expended unless and until they are needed" — plus Ringel's own concession, "Who knows what are the computing capabilities of whatever simulates us?"

Virk closes by mapping [[Thinkers/Plato]]'s cave onto the hypothesis (shadows = rendered pixels, the light source = the rendering engine, the escaped prisoner = the mystic) and offering the hypothesis as "a single framework... that brings together science and religion."

## Contradictions / Open Questions

- > [!warning] Each evidence category has a mainstream non-simulation explanation (decoherence, lattice QCD as *our* modeling artifact, mathematical structure of SUSY, holographic error correction as emergent geometry). The inference "computation-like structure → computed" risks the affirming-the-consequent pattern the vault's Phil of Science pages warn about; cf. [[Contradictions/Popper vs Kuhn - Falsification vs Paradigms]] on what would count as disconfirmation.
- The reply to the resource argument makes the hypothesis's predictions elastic — optimization can absorb any negative result.
