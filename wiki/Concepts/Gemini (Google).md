# Gemini (Google)

> Google's multimodal model family with strong emphasis on video, creation, and proactive/personal agents (post Google I/O May 2026 updates).

## Key June 2026 Capabilities (from [[Daily Advanced AI Tips - 2026-06-02]])

- **Gemini Omni**: "Create anything from any input." Particularly powerful video generation and editing. Advanced multimodal with natural conversational editing and avatar generation from selfies.
- **Gemini 3.5 Flash**: Frontier intelligence level with excellent agentic + coding performance. Fast for long-horizon tasks.
- **Gemini Spark**: 24/7 personal proactive AI agent (user-directed task management). Includes "Antigravity" for building and deploying agents, Deep Research (collab planning + viz), Managed Agents in API, native desktop app, music creation.

## Strengths
- Multimodal creation (esp. video).
- Proactive / always-on personal agents.
- Fast long-context/agentic work via Flash.
