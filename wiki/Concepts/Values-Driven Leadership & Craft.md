# Values-Driven Leadership & Craft

> Leadership and craft practiced with explicit values and moral grounding — profits and purpose are compatible; employee/community dignity and precision discipline produce sustainable excellence and legacy. Core exemplars: Howard Schultz (Starbucks values from personal family story) and the precision/generous craft across culinary and creative classes in the 2026 MasterClass collection.

## Core Lessons
- **Values and profits are not enemies** (Schultz): After seeing his injured father "tossed aside" by his employer, Schultz made employee dignity a top value. Starbucks under his leadership balanced growth with benefits, culture, and community impact. "Not every decision in business is an economic one."
- **Fiduciary + moral duty**: Leaders owe legal/ethical duty to shareholders but also a broader duty to the communities and people they serve. Regret of inaction (or values-free action) is expensive.
- **Craft as values in action**: Culinary classes (Keller, Franklin, Poilane, Ansel, Bottura, Ottolenghi, etc.) and BBQ notes emphasize respect for ingredients, waste-not, sensory precision, repetition until mastery, and generosity (sharing technique, feeding people well). The same shows up in photography (Liebovitz), documentary (Burns), music (Questlove curation), and magic (Penn & Teller skepticism + showmanship).
- **Legacy through disciplined vision + community impact**: Wintour's Met Gala reinvention and $231M+ raised for the Costume Institute; Blakely tying success to women's education/entrepreneurship philanthropy.

## Relevance to Vault
- **Career**: Risk consultant work at Alliant (helping ag clients protect what they value — land, income, legacy) is values-driven. The promotion to Sr AE is an opportunity to own a book with the same care for clients and team that Schultz modeled.
- **AI / Maintenance**: The wiki itself is a craft project — deliberate, precise, generous (cross-links for future users), values-driven (accurate provenance, no silent overwrites, compounding knowledge as a public good for the user).
- **Personal sustainability**: Craft discipline + values prevent burnout and give meaning beyond metrics (links to sleep, mindfulness, mental strength classes).
